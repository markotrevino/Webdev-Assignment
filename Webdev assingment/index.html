Delicious Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make these brownies:

  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Follow these steps to make perfect brownies:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, sugar, and vanilla. Mix well.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the cocoa, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until combined.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  8. Allow to cool before slicing. Enjoy your delicious brownies!

Bonus Tips

Tip: To make these brownies extra fudgy, reduce the baking time slightly! Don't reduce it by a lot, no one wants raw brownies!
